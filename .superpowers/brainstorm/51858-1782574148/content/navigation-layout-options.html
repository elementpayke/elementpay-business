Where should ElementPay navigation live?

Click the layout that feels closest to the direction you want. I’ll use your choice to shape the invoice creation redesign next.

A. Full Left Sidebar

Move primary dashboard navigation into a left rail, keep search/profile/actions in a top utility bar, and let invoice pages feel like focused workspaces.

B. Icon Rail

A slimmer version with icons only by default. It gives maximum canvas space, but labels require hover/tooltips or expansion.

C. Keep Top Tabs, Add Workspace Split

Least disruptive: keep the current top navigation, but make invoice creation look closer to the reference with a live preview panel.

My recommendation

  • Option A fits a finance dashboard with many recurring tools.
  • It matches the reference and makes invoice creation feel calmer.
  • It gives room for “More” items like Invoicing, Bulk Pay, Settings, and Support.

Watch-outs

  • Mobile needs a drawer or bottom bar fallback.
  • The current top tabs should be retired carefully so active states do not split attention.
  • The invoice editor still needs a responsive fallback below desktop widths.
